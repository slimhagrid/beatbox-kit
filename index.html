NEW! BAUDIT — BEATBOX AUDIT
Baudit

Upload or record up to 2 minutes of beatbox audio. Baudit detects your average BPM, maps every beat, and flags exactly where your timing rushes ahead or drags behind so you know what to fix.

  • Automatic BPM detection from your waveform
  • Beat-by-beat timing drift analysis
  • Too-fast and too-slow markers on a timeline
  • Quick-listen to any flagged moment
  • Compare two takes side-by-side
Open Baudit →
NEW! BAMPLER — BEATBOX SAMPLER
Bampler

Upload or record up to 2 minutes of beatbox audio. Bampler is a general sampler tool created for Beatbox Kit. Slice up your routines, or some of your favorite beatboxer's beats.

  • Play 16 reassignable pads with your keyboard
  • One-Shot and Lock per pad
  • Sample length editing
  • Auto Chop feature
  • Record from your mic directly to a pad
Open Bampler →
NEW! BRANULAR — GRANULAR SYNTHESIZER + SAMPLING + SEQUENCER
Branular

Upload up to three audio files (long ones get a quick 2-minute trim picker) and let Branular pull grains for you, or drag the waveform yourself to scan and spray grains live. Build evolving sequences across multiple rows, lock the keepers, and morph it all with onboard effects.

  • 16-step tracker sequencer with percussion-biased random grains
  • Up to 3 audio tracks, gathered and locked independently
  • Scrub mode — drag the waveform to scan and spray grains live
  • Per-step Lock, FX toggle, and copy/paste between steps
  • Built-in effects board, adjustable BPM, and record-to-file
Open Branular →
ABOUT BEATBOX KIT

Built for the craft.

Beatbox Kit exists because it's fun to get better at beatboxing. Create an analytical approach to improving your routines with a tool kit developed specifically for beatboxers.

Free to use.

All tools in Beatbox Kit are made for the community. There's no mandatory subscription for Beatbox Kit.
